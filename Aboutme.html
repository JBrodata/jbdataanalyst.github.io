About Me

Hello! I'm Jim and I am on an exciting journey from small landscaping business owner to full time data analyst. Deciding to make this career change while juggling my family life and a successful business has not been easy, but I am not one to shy away from a challenge.

Once I decided that I was ready to move on from my business, I started looking at various career paths. Data Analytics stood out as a career that seemed very interesting to me. I have always been a curious person and the idea of data telling a story and using that story to find the "a-ha" moment is incredibly appealing to me. I also really enjoy problem solving, which was something that I have loved about running my business for the last 14 years, and also something that drew me to data analytics.

In the rare event that I am not running my business, working on my career change, or chipping away at my own honey-do list, I also enjoy taking my family to Cape Cod or roaming the woods of NH with my 13 year old chocolate lab.
