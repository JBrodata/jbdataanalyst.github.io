About Me

Hello! I'm Jim and I am a data analyst that recently changed careers from owning a small business.

Deciding to move on my from business was not an easy decision. My business was going well and I had built up a great list of Clients. But I had achieved what I set out to do and I was burning the candle at both ends and the middle trying to keep up with the work. I decided that I was ready for a change, but it had to be something that I loved. Looking at potential career paths made me realize that I had been a data analyst for the last 14 years, so it seemed like a natural fit. I had been using SQL and Excel regularly for my business metrics and I was using data to make business decisions. It was the thing that I loved most about owning a small business. Seeing the revenue grow as predicted when I made a business change was incredibly satisfying. That is what I set out to do and I didn't stop until it happened.

I am now working as a full-time data analyst and I love getting to work with data everyday. Using SQL in a much bigger data environment allowded me to grow my skills and use SQL in ways that I wasn't able to before. I also get the opportunity to extract data from many different sources and it is always a fun challenge transforming that data into something that I need to work with. My eventual goal is to grow into an advanced data analyics role or data engineering.
